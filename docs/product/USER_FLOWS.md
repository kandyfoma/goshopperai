the# User Flows

## Overview

This document describes the key user journeys through Invoice Intelligence, including screen-by-screen flows and decision points.

## User Personas

### Primary: Marie - Cost-Conscious Shopper
- **Age**: 28-45
- **Location**: Kinshasa, DRC
- **Goals**: Track family spending, find best prices
- **Pain Points**: Doesn't know if she's overpaying, loses receipts
- **Device**: Android smartphone (mid-range)

### Secondary: Jean - Small Business Owner
- **Age**: 35-55
- **Location**: Gombe, Kinshasa
- **Goals**: Track business expenses, compare supplier prices
- **Pain Points**: Manual expense tracking is tedious
- **Device**: Android/iPhone
