# Privacy Policy for GoShopper AI

**Last Updated:** December 14, 2024  
**Effective Date:** December 14, 2024

## Introduction

Welcome to GoShopper AI ("we," "our," or "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our receipt scanning and price tracking application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information:** Email address, name, and profile picture (when using Google or Apple Sign-In)
- **Receipt Data:** Images of receipts you scan, including store names, product names, prices, and dates
- **Shopping Lists:** Items you add to your shopping lists
- **Preferences:** App settings, favorite stores, and notification preferences

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information:** Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** Features used, time spent in app, frequency of use
- **Location Data:** Approximate location (city/region) to show nearby stores (only with your permission)
- **Analytics Data:** Crash reports, performance metrics, and app errors

### Community Price Data

- **Anonymized Price Information:** When you scan receipts, we collect and anonymize product prices, store information, and purchase data
- **No Personal Identifiers:** Price data is completely anonymized and does not contain any information that could identify you personally
- **Community Benefit:** Anonymized price data helps provide better price comparisons for all users

### Camera and Photo Library

- We access your camera to scan receipts
- We access your photo library to save and retrieve receipt images
- All receipt images are stored securely and encrypted

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

1. **Core Functionality:**
   - Processing and analyzing receipt data
   - Tracking prices across different stores
   - Generating spending insights and analytics
   - Managing your shopping lists

2. **Service Improvement:**
   - Improving OCR accuracy
   - Enhancing price tracking algorithms
   - Developing new features
   - Fixing bugs and technical issues

3. **Communication:**
   - Sending price alerts and notifications
   - Providing customer support
   - Notifying you of updates and new features

4. **Security:**
   - Detecting and preventing fraud
   - Protecting against unauthorized access
   - Maintaining account security

## Your Rights and Choices

### Community Data Contribution

By default, your receipt scanning contributes to our anonymized community price database. You can control this:

- **Opt-Out:** Disable community data contribution in Settings > Privacy > Community Data
- **What Happens:** When disabled, your price data won't be shared with the community
- **Impact:** Price comparisons may be less comprehensive but still functional
- **Existing Data:** Opting out doesn't remove previously contributed anonymized data

### Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal data:

- **Account Deletion:** Delete your account through the app settings
- **Data Removal:** All personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days
- **Receipt Images:** Original receipt images are deleted immediately
- **Community Data:** Anonymized price data remains to maintain service quality

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Location
- All data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers
- Receipt images are encrypted at rest and in transit
- Authentication is handled through secure OAuth providers

### Security Measures
- End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
- Regular security audits and updates
- Secure authentication protocols
- Access controls and monitoring

### Data Retention
- Receipt data is retained as long as your account is active
- You can delete individual receipts or all data at any time
- Deleted data is permanently removed within 30 days

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We DO NOT:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your receipt data with advertisers
- Rent or lease your information
- Use your data for unrelated purposes

### We MAY Share Data With:

1. **Service Providers:**
   - Google Firebase (cloud hosting and database)
   - Google Analytics (usage analytics)
   - Crash reporting services
   
   These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for providing services to us.

2. **Legal Requirements:**
   - When required by law or legal process
   - To protect our rights or property
   - To prevent fraud or security threats
   - With your explicit consent

3. **Aggregated Data:**
   - We may share anonymized, aggregated statistics about usage patterns
   - This data cannot be used to identify individual users

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control
- **View Your Data:** Access all your stored information in the app
- **Edit Information:** Update your profile and preferences
- **Delete Data:** Remove individual receipts or delete your entire account
- **Export Data:** Download your data in a portable format

### Privacy Controls
- **Notifications:** Disable price alerts and notifications
- **Location:** Revoke location permissions at any time
- **Camera/Photos:** Manage permissions in device settings
- **Analytics:** Opt out of anonymous usage analytics

### Account Deletion
- You can delete your account at any time through the app settings
- Account deletion removes all your data permanently
- Process typically completes within 30 days
